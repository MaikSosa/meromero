Bones Bearing's Site Plan

Mike Sosa

WDD 130-6

Overview

Purpose

With the collaboration of a a good friend. I will program his wireframe for a graphic design homework. treat it as a real project for a brand of skate bearings.

Audience

The web page is a store online where skaters can buy merch and bearings. The audience can have a wide range of ages, although the majority will be teenagers and adults that love the sport. The page has to show a fresh young design that is attractive to young audience. It is meant to be the point of meeting for customers and skater fans.
